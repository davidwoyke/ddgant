## Report
We traded information and sold Mitosian antidotes to the Gold Grabblers in Renth, but our destination was the Malévol mansion in Tirth. We met Osmund and Alan of the Swans in the courtyard, and they joined us in our ascension to the second-storey window. Two giant crab spiders assailed us. The first fell to Bavaan's dextrous leaping strike, and Ethereal successfully warned the second to flee. We "expanded" the doors and windows with crowbars and other implements and enlisted the help of numerous homuncubolds, admirers of Eryndor, in removing the marble statue from the entrance hall. 

The second delve that day yielded clothing, portraits, books, a cracked emerald, and a dwarven longsword. The retainer Alan spent too much time in an illusory meditation room and aged 10 years. Tinkersack opened a gaseous urn and many doors, one which brought him face-to-face with a monstrous, acid-spitting slug. We suspect this is Jean-Giscard transformed, and have a plan to return him to normal. The delve ended when Osmund fell to a trio of troglodytes inhabiting a smoky lounge and we fled with our gains, leaving poor Osmund to the ravenous beasts.
